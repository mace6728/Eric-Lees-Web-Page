The opportunity to get into badminton.

Shortly after I entered Wuling High School, a friend told me that the badminton team was recruiting new members. Even though I had no prior experience playing badminton, the team accepted beginners as well. So, I decided to join the team and start training.

Photos from training sessions.

Resigning from the WLSH badminton team.

However, after a year of being on the team, I felt that I couldn't balance the team's practices with my schoolwork. So, I decided to resign. Even so, I still occasionally go to the badminton court to play with my friends.

Joining the NYCU badminton team

After entering university, I participated in the freshman ranking tournament organized by the NYCU badminton team. Even though I was eliminated in the second round, I successfully joined the team and have been practicing ever since.
