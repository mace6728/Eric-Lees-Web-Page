The band I like

My favorite band is Yorushika. I first heard their music in high school and was immediately captivated by the sweet and gentle voice of the lead vocalist, suis. However, after listening to several of their albums, I realized that suis's voice is highly versatile—capable of delivering powerful high notes while also conveying sincere emotions through soft, lyrical melodies. Paired with their upbeat musical style, the clean and clear arrangement composed of guitar, bass, piano, and jazz drums allows me to completely immerse myself in their music.

My favorite song

This is my favorite song, 'Kutsu no Hanabi'. It is based on the prose 'The Nighthawk Star' written by Kenji Miyazawa and fully showcases the exceptional control of suis, the lead vocalist. In the verses, suis's gentle and clear voice feels as if the nighthawk is flying to the point of exhaustion. Then, in the chorus, her powerful yet sorrowful voice feels like the nighthawk, in its final moments, uses all its strength to soar into the sky. As a listener, I am deeply captivated.
